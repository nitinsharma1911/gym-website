Workout Plans

Choose the right plan for your fitness level

Beginner Plan

Perfect for those starting their fitness journey

  • Full body workouts
  • 3 days per week
  • Focus on form & technique
  • Progressive intensity

Intermediate Plan

For those with some gym experience

  • Split routines
  • 4-5 days per week
  • Increased volume & intensity
  • Strength & hypertrophy focus

Advanced Plan

For experienced lifters seeking maximum gains

  • Specialized splits
  • 5-6 days per week
  • Advanced techniques
  • Periodization programming
Beginner Workout Plan
Plan Overview
  • Duration: 8 weeks
  • Frequency: 3 days/week
  • Session length: 45-60 minutes
  • Focus: Full body, foundation building
Key Principles
  • Focus on proper form
  • Progressive overload
  • Consistency over intensity
  • Adequate rest & recovery
Weekly Schedule
Day 1: Full Body A
Barbell Squats
3 sets of 8-10 reps | 60-90 seconds rest
Bench Press
3 sets of 8-10 reps | 60-90 seconds rest
Bent-over Rows
3 sets of 8-10 reps | 60-90 seconds rest
Shoulder Press
3 sets of 10-12 reps | 60 seconds rest
Plank
3 sets of 30-45 seconds | 30 seconds rest
Day 2: Rest or Active Recovery
Light Cardio (Optional)
20-30 minutes of walking, cycling, or swimming
Stretching
Full body stretching routine for 10-15 minutes
Day 3: Full Body B
Romanian Deadlifts
3 sets of 8-10 reps | 60-90 seconds rest
Incline Dumbbell Press
3 sets of 8-10 reps | 60-90 seconds rest
Lat Pulldowns
3 sets of 8-10 reps | 60-90 seconds rest
Dumbbell Lunges
3 sets of 10-12 reps per leg | 60 seconds rest
Leg Raises
3 sets of 12-15 reps | 30 seconds rest
Days 4-7: Rest & Recovery

Focus on nutrition, hydration, and getting adequate sleep for muscle recovery.

Important Notes:
  • Always warm up for 5-10 minutes before each workout
  • Use weights that challenge you but allow for proper form
  • Increase weight gradually as you get stronger
  • Consult a trainer if you're unsure about any exercise
Intermediate Workout Plan
Plan Overview
  • Duration: 12 weeks
  • Frequency: 4 days/week
  • Session length: 60-75 minutes
  • Focus: Strength building, hypertrophy
Key Principles
  • Progressive overload with tracking
  • Balanced push/pull training
  • Include compound & isolation work
  • Focus on nutrition to support growth

Advanced Workout Plan
Plan Overview
  • Duration: 12+ weeks
  • Frequency: 5-6 days/week
  • Session length: 75-90 minutes
  • Focus: Strength, hypertrophy & advanced techniques
Key Principles
  • Periodization (strength, volume, deload weeks)
  • Advanced techniques (supersets, drop sets)
  • Focus on weak points
  • Strict progressive overload tracking
